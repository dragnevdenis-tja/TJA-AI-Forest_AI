    *   **Deep Learning Edge**: Hybrid CRNN architecture for spatial and temporal precision.
    *   **Low Latency**: Seconds from detection to alert.
*   **Speaker Note**: "Forest Audio AI is an automated surveillance system. We don't wait for smoke; we listen for the spark. Using advanced neural networks, we identify the specific 'acoustic fingerprint' of threats in real-time."

## Slide 4: Technical Edge - The "Validator"
*   **Visual**: A "Self-Improving Loop" diagram showing the primary model and the Validator model interacting.
*   **Headline**: Beyond Standard AI: Adversarial Hardening.
*   **Key Points**:
    *   **The Validator**: A secondary AI that monitors the primary model for mistakes.
    *   **Robustness**: Built to handle rain, wind, and complex forest noise.
    *   **Reliability**: Significant reduction in false positives compared to standard models.
*   **Speaker Note**: "The forest is noisy. Wind and rain create 'false alarms' for most systems. Our proprietary 'Validator' architecture creates an adversarial feedback loop, where the system effectively teaches itself to ignore background noise and focus only on the real threats."
